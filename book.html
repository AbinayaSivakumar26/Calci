About Grand Horizon Hotel

Where luxury meets the Mediterranean

Established in 2008, Grand Horizon Hotel has been the premier destination for travelers seeking an unforgettable coastal experience. Our 5-star resort blends contemporary elegance with traditional charm.

Our Story

Founded by the Santorini family, our hotel began as a small 12-room guesthouse and has grown into one of the region's most awarded luxury resorts, while maintaining our commitment to personalized service.

Our Team

Led by General Manager Marco Bellini, our staff of 150 hospitality professionals is dedicated to making your stay exceptional.
